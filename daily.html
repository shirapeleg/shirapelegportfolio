Illustrations

DAILY

This collection features three distinct illustrative projects, each exploring different techniques and themes. The first is a concertina book created for Eduardo Berti's story, "Double Life," rendered using stencils and spray paint. The second series captures the wide emotional spectrum of a devoted soccer fan throughout a match - from anticipation and excitement to frustration and despair - using pencils and pastels. Finally, the third piece consists of rapid, black-pen sketches that document fleeting moments and observations from daily life.
