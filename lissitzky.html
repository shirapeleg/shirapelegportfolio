LISSITZKY

Posters based on assembly instruction formats that have been abstracted, created as a tribute to El Lissitzky. The use of technical illustration reflects Lissitzky's focus on structures, disassembly, and reconstruction, turning everyday instructions into a visual exploration of his artistic values.

70*100 cm, Digital processing of assembly instructions, 2023
