LUCID DREAMS

View website

This website is an interactive experience designed to guide the user through a full 24-hour cycle, from morning to the following morning, through 6 structured tasks aimed at inducing a lucid dream. As the user scrolls, they move through the layers of everyday reality, gradually transitioning into the dream world, and eventually returning back to waking life. The scrolling becomes both a narrative and spatial device, transforming the passage of time into a visual and experiential journey between consciousness and subconsciousness.
