Illustrated Book

THE PRINCESS WILL COME AT FOUR

This illustrated book presents my interpretation of "The Princess Will Come at Four" by Wolfdietrich Schnurre, reimagined in a local Israeli context. The story unfolds in Ramat Gan, with a particular focus on the Ramat Gan Safari - where an aging zookeeper forms an unexpected connection with a hyena. Through this relationship, the tale explores themes of loneliness, routine, and companionship. The illustrations weave together scenes from the urban landscape of Ramat Gan, moments inside the Safari, and glimpses of the typical Israeli home.

15 pages, 17*24 cm, black pen and digital coloring in Photoshop, indigo print, hebrew, 2025
