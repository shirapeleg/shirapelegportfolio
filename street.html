Motion

STREET

This motion design project explores the experience of the street by focusing on the everyday elements we usually pass without noticing. From crosswalks and traffic lights to overhead power lines, the work brings attention to the small, familiar details that shape our urban environment. Using a grid of 9 rectangles, these overlooked fragments are isolated, framed, and set into motion, shifting them from the background to the center of attention.

1350*1080 px, footage and after effects, 2025
