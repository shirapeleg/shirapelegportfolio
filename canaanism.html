Motion Posters

CANAANISM

The Canaanites were a radical cultural movement in mid-20th-century Israel that sought to redefine local identity through language and ancient Near-Eastern heritage rather than religion. This project was created for a special 6 hour broadcast of music and dialogue at Radio Mehoga, exploring the movement's questions and their resonance today. The project features five motion posters, each dedicated to a different hour of the broadcast, along with a comprehensive event program. Additionally, it includes a documentary format that captures the visual evolution of the sculptures, tracing their development from the project's inception to the final result.
